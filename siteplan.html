Perfect Diet – Site Plan

Site Name

Perfect Diet

This name was chosen because it clearly reflects the purpose of the website — helping users create their own "perfect diet" based on their food preferences and goals. It’s easy to remember, professional, and reflects a healthy lifestyle.

Optional domain availability: perfectdietplan.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of Perfect Diet is to provide users with personalized meal plans based on the foods they like, along with weekly recipe ideas that make staying on a diet easier and more enjoyable. Our goal is to help users reach their health goals (weight loss, muscle gain, balanced eating) in a way that is flexible, tasty, and sustainable.

Scenarios

  • How can I get a customized diet plan with foods I enjoy?
  • What healthy recipes can I try this week to stay motivated?

Color Schema

The site will use the following color scheme:

  • Green (#178606): Used for headers, buttons, and accents to convey health and freshness.
  • White (#fff): Used as a soft background color and for some UI elements.

Typography

  • Headings: 'Joan', bold sans-serif for strong section titles.
  • Body Text: Cabin, clean and easy to read for all general content.
